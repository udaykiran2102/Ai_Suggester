AI Trip Planner

If you are looking for a way to plan your perfect trip in just a few clicks, you might want to check out this AI website: https://www.buildai.space/app/dae3da25-888e-448f-b15c-5a20ca4ca961. This website allows you to enter your desired travel destination and the number of days you want to spend there, and then it generates a detailed schedule for you based on your input. You can see the attractions, activities, and transportation options for each day of your trip, as well as the estimated costs and distances. You can also customize the appearance of your itinerary and share it with others.
